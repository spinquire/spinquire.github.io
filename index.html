Spinquire Research Initiative

Spinquire is an independent research unit analyzing the inner workings of crypto gambling platforms. Our work focuses on transparency failures, stablecoin-fueled behavioral risks, and regulatory grey zones.

What We Investigate

  • Stablecoin loss acceleration and frictionless addiction patterns
  • Gambling platforms using "provably fair" as a shield, not a system
  • KYC circumvention and unlicensed operator behavior
  • User manipulation via bonus complexity and withdrawal friction

Our Ethos

We are not affiliated with any gambling operator. We publish under no commercial incentive. Our output is written by humans, reviewed for bias, and structured for open access. If a platform manipulates trust, we report it.
