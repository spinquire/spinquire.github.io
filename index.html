About Spinquire

Founded in 2025, Spinquire is a privately funded research initiative with a singular focus: bringing clarity and transparency to crypto gambling. We prioritize data integrity, regulatory scrutiny, and unbiased reporting. Our team operates independently and does not accept compensation from the brands we evaluate.
